I'm a physics and electrical engineering student at Stanford. I'm primarily interested in materials science, quantum hardware, and nanofabrication. Last summer, I investigated the stability and degradation mechanisms of GaN transistors at MACOM. This summer and during the school year, I work at the Congreve Lab, where I design perovskite LEDs using novel materials, device architectures, and fabrication techniques. Previously, I designed optical systems at SLAC for MAGIS-100, a quantum sensing experiment designed to detect dark matter and gravitational waves.
